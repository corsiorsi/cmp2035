New Orleans Dance Festival and Conference

May 18-20, 2016

A 3-day conference dedicated to all things dance! Including workshops, performances and talks. Come take class, hear from amazing choreographers and watch fantastic performances. Presentations on "Dance in the Community", given by John Badexample, artistic director of the New Orleans Dance Company, "Arts and the Spirit of New Orleans", given by Jane Doe, director of programming for the New Orleans Perfoming Arts Center and "Performing with Improv", given by Alex Noname, director of One Time Only Dance Company. Workshops include Laban Technique, Musical Theatre Jazz, Contact improvisaiton and many more! Each day has a short break from 1-2. Performance by the New Orleans Dance Company on Friday the 20 at 6 pm
