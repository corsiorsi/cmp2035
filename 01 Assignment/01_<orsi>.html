Porter Brownies

Ingredients
  • 1 Cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/4 Cup unsweetened Cocoa Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Kosher Salt
  • 3 1/2 oz semi sweet chocolate
  • 8 tbsp brown butter
  • 1/2 cup stout, porter or other dark beer
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

    • DIRECTIONS

    1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
    2. Sift together flour and cocoa powder
    3. Melt the chocolate
    4. In a small pan over medium heat, melt the butter until it just turns golden brown.
    5. Pour the brown butter into the bowl of chocolate, and add the beer.
    6. Beat together sugar and eggs until thick and shiny- 2 minutes
    7. Alternately add flour mixture and chocolate mixture.
    8. Add vanilla.
    9. Fold in chocolate chips.
    10. Pour into a greased 9 x 13 inch pan.
    11. Bake for 40-45 minutes
